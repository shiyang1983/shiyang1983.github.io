About Me

I am a Senior Staff Research Scientist Manager at Meta Platforms, leading a team in Core AI focused on audio, speech, and language technologies. My research interests lie in LLM and generative AI in audio, speech and language domains.


I am particularly focused on leveraging LLMs to empower various devices — such as mobile phones, smartwatches, smart speakers, and AR/VR systems — with general intelligence. The goal is to enable these devices to understand users and provide contextually aware services.

Search Ranking

Advanced Bing document ranking with Deep Structured Semantic Models (DSSM), including curriculum training, multilingual word hashing, online feature extraction, and post-caption semantic similarity.
